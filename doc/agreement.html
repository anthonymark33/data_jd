Jd Commercial Support Agreement

Commercial users must have a Jd Commercial Support Agreement (CSA) with Jsoftware.

A CSA provides high-priority, direct access to the J/Jd development and support team.

CSA fees are in US dollars, annual, and payable in advance.

CSA fees are for a single company with a designated primary contact who is familiar with J, Jd, and the applications. A company with diverse applications covered by different contacts needs a separate CSA for each contact.

CSA fees can be terminated after the second payment. Termination notice must be given before a payment is due. After termination, the company can continue to use Jd with the same support available to non-commercial users.

The J forums provide first line support to all Jd users.

Premium

Premium fee: $12,000

Premium includes unrestricted use of the C source for the Jd binary (jd.dll/so/dylib) that is at the heart of Jd performance. The C source for the Jd binary is not included in the JAL distribution of Jd and is available only to Premium users.

Standard

Standard fee: $6,000

Reduced

Reduced fee: $2,000

A company can provide justification and request a reduced fee. The reduced fee is available at the discretion of Jsoftware. A reduced fee automatically increases to the Standard fee after two payments unless an extension is requested and granted.

Keeping it real simple

Jsoftware provides no CSA paperwork or boilerplate. If you require more formality, we'll consider anything you provide.

Just send us an email that indicates company, contact info, designated primary contact, and CSA type.

In response we will send an invoice for the CSA and you can start commercial use of Jd as soon as we receive payment.
