Jd License

Commercial use of Jd requires a Jd License.

Non-commerical use of Jd is free and does not require a Jd License. This encourages use of Jd and will help build the talent pool of J programmers familiar with Jd. Their active participation in the J forums will provide depth and experience in free Jd support.

License fees are in USD, annual, and payable in advance.

Jd Standard License

A Jd Standard License allows commercial use of Jd on a machine. Jd can be installed multiple times on the machine and it can run in multiple instances and on multiple cores.

Jd Standard License: $8000.

Jd Special License

In some cases Jsoftware will grant a Jd Special License to encourage use of Jd where there is mutual benefit. A company can provide justification and request a Jd Special License. This is available at the discretion of Jsoftware. A Jd Special License allows Jd to be installed on 32 machines.
